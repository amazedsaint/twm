Circuit Netlist Repair

Candidates are acyclic Boolean netlists. The hard verifier compares the complete target truth table, emits a mismatch residual, and only commits a repaired gate operator after full re-verification.

The template fixes g0 as XOR and treats g1 as a mutable binary Boolean operator. The residual can identify the unique op mask that restores the target truth table.
